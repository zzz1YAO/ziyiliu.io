About Me

I am a third-year undergraduate student in Artificial Intelligence at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). I work on multimodal reasoning, long video understanding, and large language models. I am a co-first author of LongVideoAgent: Multi-Agent Reasoning with Long Videos.

Research Interests

Multimodal Reasoning

Learning to reason across video, image, and text for grounded decision making.

Long Video Understanding

Efficiently modeling long video contexts with agentic planning and memory.

Large Language Models

Investigating reasoning, alignment, and tool use for multimodal agents.
