About Me

I am an incoming MPhil student in Artificial Intelligence at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen). Currently, I am completing my senior year as an undergraduate student in AI at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT).

My research focuses on multimodal reasoning, long video understanding, and large language models.

Research Interests

Multimodal Reasoning

Learning to reason across video, image, and text for grounded decision making.

Long Video Understanding

Efficiently modeling long video contexts with agentic planning and memory.

Large Language Models

Investigating reasoning, alignment, and tool use for multimodal agents.
